iPhone 15 Pro Max Battery Life Exceeds Expectations in Latest Tests

Apple iPhone Battery

In comprehensive battery tests conducted over the past week, the iPhone 15 Pro Max has demonstrated exceptional battery performance that surpasses Apple's official claims and previous generation models.

Our testing methodology included real-world usage scenarios such as video streaming, gaming, web browsing, and social media usage. The results were consistently impressive across all categories.

Key Findings:

  • Video playback: 28 hours (vs. 29 hours claimed)
  • Web browsing: 22 hours (vs. 20 hours claimed)
  • Gaming: 12 hours of continuous gameplay
  • Mixed usage: Full day with 35% remaining

The A17 Pro chip's efficiency improvements combined with the larger battery capacity make this the best iPhone for battery life to date.
