# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

**Project Name:** DiscussionSpot
**Version:** 2.1
**Date:** October 2025
**Prepared by:** Uzair

---

## 1. Introduction

**Vision:**
DiscussionSpot is a modern online discussion platform designed to connect people around shared interests. It provides topic-based communities, post sharing, comments, voting, and multimedia support while being optimized for engagement, discoverability, and scalability.

**Goals:**

* Build a **user-friendly forum platform** with communities and categories.
* Support rich content types (text, images, video, polls).
* Enable **social engagement features** such as voting, awards, notifications.
* Integrate **Python-powered AI services** for moderation, recommendations, SEO, and analytics.
* Ensure **scalability, performance, and SEO-friendliness**.

---

## 2. Scope

### In Scope

* User authentication & profiles.
* Communities and categories management.
* Post creation (text, media, polls).
* Comments, replies, voting.
* Tags, SEO metadata, awards system.
* Real-time notifications.
* Python-based AI services (moderation, SEO, analytics).

### Out of Scope (Phase 1)

* Direct messaging between users.
* Marketplace/commerce features.
* Mobile app (future release).

---

## 3. User Roles

* **Guest**: Browse content, view posts and comments.
* **Registered User**: Join communities, create posts, comment, vote, receive notifications.
* **Moderator**: Manage community posts, rules, and members.
* **Admin**: Manage entire platform (categories, SEO, global settings).

---

## 4. Functional Requirements

### 4.1 User Accounts & Profiles

* **FR-101:** Users can sign up via email, Google, or Facebook.
* **FR-102:** Users can reset passwords through a “Forgot Password” flow.
* **FR-103:** Users can manage their profiles (avatar, banner, bio, website link).
* **FR-104:** Users can view other profiles with post history, comments, karma.
* **FR-105:** Admins can assign “Verified” badges to trusted users.
* **FR-106:** Profiles display user’s join date, last active time, awards, and achievements.
* **FR-107:** Karma score updates based on community engagement.

---

### 4.2 Communities & Categories

* **FR-201:** Users can browse communities under categories and subcategories.
* **FR-202:** Users can create communities with a name, description, banner, icon, and rules.
* **FR-203:** Communities can be public, private, or restricted.
* **FR-204:** Community admins can manage membership (add/remove members, assign moderators).
* **FR-205:** Community statistics (member count, post count) update in real-time.
* **FR-206:** Communities can be associated with categories for organization.
* **FR-207:** Deleted or banned communities should retain archived posts for audit purposes.

---

### 4.3 Posts & Media

* **FR-301:** Users can create posts of type text, link, image, video, or poll.
* **FR-302:** Posts must support rich text formatting (bold, links, lists, code blocks).
* **FR-303:** Media uploads should support images, videos, and documents with captions.
* **FR-304:** Polls allow multiple choice, with options set by the creator.
* **FR-305:** Posts can be pinned, locked, marked NSFW, or as spoilers by moderators.
* **FR-306:** Each post should track views, votes, comments, and awards.
* **FR-307:** Post slugs should be unique per community for SEO purposes.

---

### 4.4 Comments

* **FR-401:** Users can post comments and threaded replies.
* **FR-402:** Users can edit or delete their own comments.
* **FR-403:** Comments can be upvoted/downvoted.
* **FR-404:** Comments display time created, last updated, and edited status.
* **FR-405:** Moderators can remove or lock comment threads.
* **FR-406:** Comment nesting supports up to a configurable depth (default 5 levels).

---

### 4.5 Voting & Awards

* **FR-501:** Users can upvote or downvote posts and comments.
* **FR-502:** Vote score updates immediately and affects post ranking.
* **FR-503:** Users can give awards (free or paid) to posts and comments.
* **FR-504:** Awards should display icons and grant karma points to the receiver.
* **FR-505:** Award messages (optional notes) are displayed with awards.

---

### 4.6 Notifications

* **FR-601:** Users receive notifications for replies, mentions, votes, awards, and moderator actions.
* **FR-602:** Notifications can be marked as read/unread.
* **FR-603:** Notification preferences can be managed at the community level (all, important, none).
* **FR-604:** Notifications should appear in real-time if the user is online.

---

### 4.7 Tags & SEO

* **FR-701:** Users can tag posts with multiple relevant tags.
* **FR-702:** SEO metadata (title, description, keywords) can be customized for posts and communities.
* **FR-703:** Canonical URLs, Open Graph (OG), and Twitter card data should be supported.
* **FR-704:** Python microservice can auto-generate SEO suggestions when creating posts/communities.

---

### 4.8 Moderation & Safety

* **FR-801:** Moderators can remove posts, comments, and ban users from communities.
* **FR-802:** Users can report posts or comments for review.
* **FR-803:** NSFW and Spoiler flags hide sensitive content behind warnings.
* **FR-804:** Python AI service scans new posts/comments and flags harmful/spam content.
* **FR-805:** Audit logs track moderator actions for accountability.

---

### 4.9 Python-Powered Features

* **FR-901:** AI Moderation microservice flags toxic, spam, or NSFW content.
* **FR-902:** Recommendation engine suggests communities and posts to users.
* **FR-903:** SEO microservice generates suggested meta titles, keywords, and descriptions.
* **FR-904:** Analytics microservice provides insights (active users, trending tags, sentiment analysis).
* **FR-905:** Semantic search microservice improves search accuracy with AI embeddings.

---

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

* **NFR-101:** Handle at least 10,000 concurrent users.
* **NFR-102:** Response time for key actions (posting, commenting, voting) should be < 1 second.
* **NFR-103:** Secure with HTTPS, role-based access, CSRF/XSS protection.
* **NFR-104:** Python microservices must be decoupled and scalable.
* **NFR-105:** UI must be mobile-responsive.
* **NFR-106:** SEO best practices applied globally.

---

## 6. Success Metrics

* Daily active users.
* Average posts and comments per user.
* Percentage of spam removed automatically.
* Organic traffic growth.
* Retention rate (returning users).
